Cannelloni recipe

Description

Cannelonni are tube pasta stuffed with seasoned meat covered with sauce and cooked in the oven.

We'll first view all the ingredients we need then we will make them step by step.

Ingredients

  • A box of cannelonni pasta
  • 500 grams of grounded beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 oignon
  • A pinch of parsley
  • A pinch of cumin
  • A can of tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 220Â°C
  2. Mince the onion
  3. Mix the grounded beef with the egg, the minced onion, the parsley and the cumin.
  4. Stuff the tube pasta with your preparation
  5. Dispose the stuffed tubes inside an oven plate
  6. Cover all the pasta with tomato sauce
  7. Once the oven is at the right temperature, put your plate in it for 45 minutes
