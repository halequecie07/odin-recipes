Basil pasta recipe

Decription

Basil pasta is a meditteranean dish which is simple and very tasty.

This dish is appreciated because of the easiness of its recipe and the short list of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • A box of your favorite pasta
  • 50 leaves of fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • A bit of garlic
  • Grated emmental or parmiggiano (according to your preferences)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan to cook your pasta
  2. In a mixer, put all your ingredients (besides the pasta of course) and mix it
  3. Put less or more of any ingredients according to your preferences
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, mix it directly with your mixture
  5. Wait a few minutes to let the mixture melt with the pasta
