Carbonara pasta recipe

Description

Carbonara pasta are originally made with parmiggiano cheese, egg yolk, pepper and pancetta.

Here we're going to make a discount and simple version of it.

Ingredients

  • A box of your favorite pasta
  • 100 grams of minced bacon
  • 20 centiliters of light cream
  • Parmiggiano cheese
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan to cook your pasta
  2. Put your bacon in a pan to fry them
  3. Lower the heat and add the cream to reduce it
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, dip it inside your cream and bacon mix
  5. Mix it and dress in a plate with parmiggiano cheese
